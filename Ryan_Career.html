History of Species Migration

    Reynolds grew up in Canada, but, he moved to Florida for about a year in 1990 when he landed a role in a Nickelodeon show "Fifteen." After the show ended, he moved back to Vancouver until 1997 when he and a friend moved to LA to take a chance on Hollywood. After he married Blake Lively, Ryan moved with her to a small town north of Bedford New York where he currently resides.
